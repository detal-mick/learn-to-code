The Connection
Verb + -ing forms can live inside complex noun phrases. These two grammar points are not separate — they combine.
⚡ The key insight
When a verb + -ing appears after a preposition inside a noun phrase, it becomes the post-modifier. This is one of the most common patterns in formal environmental writing.
